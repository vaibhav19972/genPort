iOS / Swift IoT Smart Home

Google Home & Nest

Contributed to the Google Home iOS application — a flagship smart home platform with over 100 million downloads. Delivered 30+ user-facing features, optimized device setup flows for Nest thermostats and Chromecast, and improved application performance by reducing feature response time by 20%.

The Challenge

The Google Home app serves as the central hub for millions of IoT devices worldwide. The challenge was to maintain and enhance a massive, legacy codebase while delivering new features at scale — ensuring seamless device onboarding for Nest thermostats, Chromecast, and other smart home devices with zero friction for users.

Key Contributions

  • 30+ Feature Deliveries: Shipped over 30 user-facing features spanning device setup, home automation routines, and device management screens.
  • Nest & Chromecast Setup Optimization: Re-engineered the device onboarding flows reducing setup failure rates and improving first-time success metrics.
  • Performance Optimization: Identified and resolved performance bottlenecks, reducing feature response time by 20% through profiling and caching strategies.
  • MVVM & MVC Architecture: Worked within both architectural patterns across different modules, maintaining consistency across the codebase.
  • Cross-Team Collaboration: Collaborated with Google's internal teams on API integrations, ensuring alignment between iOS and backend services.
